# Code of conduct

We are committed to a welcoming, respectful and harassment-free community.

Be constructive, assume good intent, respect privacy, and focus technical
discussion on observable behavior. Do not pressure users to publish camera
feeds, account data or other private diagnostic material.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, discrimination, threats, deliberate
disclosure of private information, and sustained disruption of project spaces.
Maintainers may edit, remove or reject contributions and may temporarily or
permanently restrict participation when necessary to protect the community.

Report conduct concerns privately to the repository owner. Reports will be
handled as confidentially as practical.
